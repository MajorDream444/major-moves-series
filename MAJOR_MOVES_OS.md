# Major Moves OS

## Mission

Architect a 12-month publishing and operating system that transforms visual seeds into a full content, product, and curriculum ecosystem.

This repository is the source of truth for the Major Moves Series.

## Core Thesis

We are not building random lead magnets. We are building visual manifestos.

Each visual manifesto contains:

- A hook
- An emotion
- A philosophy
- A transformation
- An implied system

The book explains what the audience already feels when they see the image.

## The 1:12 Asset Ratio

Each hero image should become:

1. Short e-book, 25 to 40 pages
2. Implementation workbook
3. Ten quote cards
4. Five carousel posts
5. Podcast episode
6. YouTube lesson
7. Workshop outline
8. Keynote outline
9. AI voice lesson
10. Gumroad product listing
11. Substack essay
12. Community challenge

Twelve volumes over twelve months creates 144 core assets.

## Three-Tier Publishing Model

### Tier 1: Manifestos
Free or low-cost short visual e-books. These act as the front door.

### Tier 2: Pillar Books
Longer 100 to 200 page books that deepen the framework.

### Tier 3: Master Collection
The full Major Moves operating system integrating all volumes, workbooks, and learning tools.

## Doctrine Rules

The writing should be:

- Direct
- Practical
- Grounded
- Visionary
- Culturally aware
- Short-paragraph driven
- Clear enough for a beginner
- Strong enough for a founder

Influence: Napoleon Hill's emphasis on definite purpose, desire, organized planning, mastermind energy, faith, and persistence.

Modernization: AI-native sovereignty, automation, community ownership, personal operating systems, and legacy.

Never copy source language. Translate the spirit into original Major AI Mindset doctrine.
