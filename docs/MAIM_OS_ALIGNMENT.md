# Major Moves Series + Major AI Mindset OS Alignment

## Purpose

This document explains how the Major Moves Series connects to the broader Major AI Mindset OS.

Major Moves is not separate from MAIM. It is one publishing and doctrine engine inside the larger Major AI Mindset operating system.

## Parent System

Major AI Mindset exists to help everyday builders understand AI without shame, fear, or confusion.

Core belief:

> If you can text, you can use AI.

Core tagline:

> Likkle by likkle, we build the future.

## Major Moves Role

Major Moves turns visual manifestos into short books, workbooks, lessons, Substack essays, Gumroad products, NotebookLM study packs, and community challenges.

It is the deeper doctrine layer for builders who move from beginner awareness into systems, automation, leverage, and legacy.

Strategy starts in GitHub.
Design happens in Claude Design.
Implementation happens in Claude Code / Codex.
